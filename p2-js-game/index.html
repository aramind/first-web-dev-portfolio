Welcome to "OOO:Outsmarting Offworld Oppressors"...


...the game where you must save humanity from an unknown alien invasion.

Each batch of invaders arrives in groups of four capsules, linked together with one of these capsules acting as the controller.

For each capsule that successfully lands on Earth, 1000 humans will be lost as each invader requires a human body to take form.

As the chosen defender of Earth, your task is to create a machine that has the power to annihilate the invaders in an instant.

To do this, you must collect 1000 points by successfully destroying the control capsules.

Each time a group of capsules falls, you must quickly identify and target the control capsule.

If you target the correct capsule, it will destroy the other three, earning you points.

However, if you target the wrong one, that one capsule will be destroyed but the other three will become indestructible and hasten their landing.

To destroy the targeted capsule, click on the button in line with the falling capsule.

Win the game by collecting 1000 points before all humans are sacrificed. Good luck, defender of Earth!

Can you save humanity from the alien invasion?
